About Me

A little about Robert.

I'm a Software Engineer based in Boston with over five years of experience building tools for internal threat and security teams. I joined Carbon Black in 2019, which was subsequently acquired by VMware and then Broadcom, giving me the rare experience of navigating two major acquisitions while continuing to ship meaningful work. In that time I've worked across the stack designing and maintaining a fullstack data processing pipeline in Golang and React, building compilers for custom domain-specific languages, and owning CI/CD infrastructure end to end. What I enjoy most is finding solutions to the inefficiencies that slow teams down so the people around me can spend their time on the work that actually matters.

I also really value my comedic pursuits. There is no greater joy in my life than knowing that something I did on stage made an audience member laugh and have a good night. I have performed in improv comedy house teams at Improv Asylum in the North End of Boston for a few years now. Additionally I've spent the past few months learning about clowning and physical theater to add more tools to comedy repetoire. I want to continue doing comedy in and around Boston, and aim to produce a show at some point in 2026.

Separately I also wanted to call out my love of both video games and board games. I love exploring worlds and hearing stories that challenge my own perspective and give me an opportunity to learn something new. In the future I will make a page that shows off my favorite games and shows.
